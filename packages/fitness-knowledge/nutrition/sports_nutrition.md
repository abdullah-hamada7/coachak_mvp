# Sports Nutrition Fundamentals

## Macronutrient Guidelines

### Protein
- General fitness: 1.6-2.0 g/kg body weight
- Hypertrophy/strength: 1.8-2.2 g/kg
- Fat loss: 2.0-2.4 g/kg (muscle preservation)
- Distribute across 3-5 meals, 25-40g per meal

### Carbohydrates
- Moderate activity: 3-5 g/kg
- High activity/strength: 4-7 g/kg
- Prioritize around training sessions

### Fat
- Minimum 0.5 g/kg for hormonal health
- Target 25-35% of total calories
- Emphasize unsaturated fats

## Goal Adjustments

- **Fat loss**: TDEE minus 300-500 calories
- **Hypertrophy**: TDEE plus 200-300 calories
- **Strength**: TDEE plus 100-200 calories

## Hydration

- 30-40 ml per kg body weight daily
- Add 500-1000ml per hour of intense exercise

## Meal Timing

- Pre-workout: carbs + moderate protein, 1-3 hours before
- Post-workout: protein 20-40g within 2 hours
- Consistent meal timing supports habit formation
