# Progressive Overload Principles

Progressive overload is the gradual increase of stress placed on the body during training. Key methods:

1. **Increase load**: Add 2.5-5kg when all sets hit target reps at RPE 8 or below
2. **Increase volume**: Add 1 set per exercise every 2-3 weeks if recovery permits
3. **Increase frequency**: Move from 2x to 3x weekly for lagging muscle groups
4. **Increase ROM**: Deepen squat depth, extend range on curls

## Safety Guidelines

- Never increase load and volume simultaneously
- Cap weekly volume increases at 10%
- Deload every 4th week: reduce volume by 40-50%
- Beginners should focus on movement quality before load progression

## RPE Scale

- RPE 6: Could do 4+ more reps
- RPE 7: Could do 3 more reps
- RPE 8: Could do 2 more reps (ideal working set intensity)
- RPE 9: Could do 1 more rep
- RPE 10: Maximum effort
