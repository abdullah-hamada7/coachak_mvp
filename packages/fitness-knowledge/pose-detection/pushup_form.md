# Push-Up Form Analysis Guide

## Key Joint Angles

- **Elbow angle at bottom**: 70-90 degrees
- **Body alignment**: Shoulder-hip-ankle should form straight line (deviation > 15 degrees = sagging or piking)
- **Elbow flare**: Elbows at 30-45 degrees from torso

## Common Mistakes

1. **Hip sag**: Hips drop below shoulder-ankle line — cue "squeeze glutes, brace core"
2. **Partial ROM**: Elbow angle > 100 at bottom — cue "chest to floor"
3. **Elbow flare**: Elbows > 60 degrees — cue "elbows at 45 degrees"

## Rep Counting

- Bottom: Elbow angle < 90 degrees
- Top: Elbow angle > 160 degrees with body aligned
