# Bicep Curl Form Analysis Guide

## Key Joint Angles

- **Elbow angle at top**: 30-50 degrees (full contraction)
- **Elbow angle at bottom**: 160-180 degrees (full extension)
- **Elbow stability**: Upper arm should remain vertical (swing > 20 degrees forward = cheating)

## Common Mistakes

1. **Incomplete ROM**: Not extending fully at bottom — cue "full extension at bottom"
2. **Momentum/swing**: Elbow moves forward during curl — cue "keep elbows pinned"
3. **Shoulder elevation**: Shoulders rise during curl — cue "keep shoulders down"

## Rep Counting

- Concentric complete: Elbow angle < 50 degrees
- Eccentric complete: Elbow angle > 150 degrees
